About Me

Technology Architecture Consultant & Software Developer

I was born in South Korea and moved back and forth between Korea and Michigan, until in 2011, my family decided to settle in Michigan for good. Fast forward 8 years, I graduated from Michigan State University with a B.S. in Computer Science and Minor in Mathematics.

After graduating, I moved to Chicago, Illinois to work as a Technical Architecture Delivery Analyst at Accenture. In the process, I stepped my feet into a wide spectrum of roles across different types of sectors/industries (Public Service, Sales Tech, Field Service Tech) and projects (Customer Service, Salesforce, ServiceMax, AI Chatbots). Currently, I am a chatbot experience design lead, which involves leading client calls to discuss/design content that the chatbot can provide, defect testing, and performing data analysis/reporting for chatbot performance metrics.

In the future, I wish to utilize my software development skills that I acquired during my Undergraduate studies by transitioning into a more technical role in areas related to Big Data Analysis/Machine Learning/AI Ethics.

During my free time, I enjoy taking long walks near Chicago rivers with some good music, visiting different cafes, and baking.
